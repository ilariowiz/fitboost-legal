Privacy Policy

FitBoost Last updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FitBoost (“FitBoost”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

Key point: FitBoost can be used in Demo Mode. When you request an AI analysis, we may send limited profile-related information to a third-party AI provider (OpenAI) to generate insights. We will ask for your permission before sending data for AI processing.

1) Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to users of the FitBoost mobile app and any related services we provide.

2) Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Support communications: if you contact us (e.g., email), we may collect the content of your messages and contact details you provide.
  • Subscription-related information: if you subscribe, we receive information about your subscription status (e.g., active/expired), but we do not receive your full payment card details.

2.2 Social Profile Data (TikTok / Social Platforms)

If you connect a social account or request an analysis, we may process information such as:

  • Public profile identifiers (e.g., username/handle)
  • Public profile details (e.g., display name, profile photo URL, bio) where available
  • Public metrics and performance data (e.g., follower counts, engagement, video stats) where available

We do not collect your social platform password. We do not request or store your login credentials.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and app usage data: app events (e.g., screens viewed, features used), device type, OS version, language, and approximate region.
  • Identifiers: a random app user identifier (for example, an anonymous authentication identifier) to operate the Service.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and performance data to improve reliability.

3) How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the Service (including Demo Mode and account features)
  • To generate analytics and AI-based insights requested by you
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • To manage subscriptions and entitlements (e.g., Pro features)
  • To communicate with you about support requests and important updates

4) AI Processing (Third-Party AI Services)

FitBoost uses artificial intelligence to generate insights, summaries, and suggestions. When you request an AI analysis, certain information may be sent to a third-party AI provider for processing.

4.1 What Data May Be Sent for AI Processing

  • Public username/handle (e.g., TikTok username)
  • Public profile details and publicly available metrics (e.g., engagement and video statistics)
  • Aggregated performance information derived from public profile data

4.2 What We Do Not Send

  • Social platform passwords or login credentials
  • Private messages
  • Non-public account data (unless you explicitly authorize access through official platform permissions, where applicable)

4.3 Who the Data Is Sent To

The AI service provider used by FitBoost is OpenAI.

You can review OpenAI’s privacy policy here: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

4.4 User Permission (Consent)

Before sending data to the AI provider for analysis, FitBoost clearly informs you about what data will be sent, identifies the provider, and asks for your permission. You may decline; in that case the AI analysis will not run.

5) Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only as described below:

5.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate the Service, such as:

  • Analytics and crash reporting (to understand usage and fix issues)
  • Backend infrastructure (to store data and run server functions)
  • Subscription management (to verify entitlements)
  • AI processing (OpenAI) when you request AI analysis and provide consent

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of FitBoost, our users, or others.

6) Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. We may retain aggregated or de-identified information for analytics and product improvement.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8) Your Choices and Rights

  • Consent for AI: you can decline AI processing when prompted.
  • Access / deletion: you may request access to or deletion of certain data, subject to legal and operational requirements.
  • Disconnect social accounts: if available in the app, you can disconnect connected accounts.

To make a request, contact us using the details in the “Contact Us” section below.

9) Children’s Privacy

FitBoost is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10) International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps designed to ensure adequate protections for cross-border data transfers.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice within the app.
