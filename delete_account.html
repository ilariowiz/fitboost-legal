Delete Your FitBoost Account

If you would like to request the deletion of your FitBoost account and associated data, please follow the instructions below:

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to: turn.appsolution@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address or TikTok username

What data will be deleted

  • User account information (Firebase UID)
  • Stored TikTok profile data
  • Usage and analytics data associated with your account

Data retention

We may retain certain information if required by law (e.g. billing records related to Google Play subscriptions).

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
